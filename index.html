    Welcome to miKitchen

    Explore, Cook, and Learn

    miKitchen is your interactive kitchen companion. Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure, experiment with new recipes, and discover the world of cooking and nutrition.

    Getting Started:

    1. Fridge: Click on the fridge to input your ingredients. Simply type ingredients into the notepad, then click "Add Ingredient" These ingredients will be used in recipe generation.
    2. Drawer: Click on the drawer to refine your recipe choices. Customize your culinary experience by setting dietary preferences, cuisine types, and more.
    3. Stove: Click on the stove icon to open the cookbook. Click the "Generate Recipe" button to generate a recipe and unveil its secrets, including a macronutrient breakdown.
    4. Dishwasher: Need a fresh start? Click on the dishwasher icon to reset all appliances and start over.

    Ready to Cook?

    miKitchen is your gateway to delicious recipes and a world of culinary knowledge. Bon appetit!
